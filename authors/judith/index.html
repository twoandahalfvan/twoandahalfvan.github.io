Judith

Hi, I am Judith. I love everything about strolling through new places and getting inspired by people. When I travel my itinerary often centers around plant-based foodie heavens, unique shops that happen to offer coffee, and green parks or sport activities. I would probably trade culture and coffee shops for living amongst cows and donkeys on a farm sanctuary. Besides food and animals, I also like writing and learning new things.
