Individual sized shower tub

Dominik

This 1.5mm thick stainless steel shower tub is built according to your dimensions, which mean it will take a while. You can specify the number and position of the drainage points.

You will need additional 1.5mm thick stainless steel sheets for the walls of the shower. You can source these sheets from your local metal shop.

If you need more info on how to build this type of shower, take a look at our article on [how to build a space saving shower]

DACH: https://shop.versandmetall.de/de/wohnmobil-zubehoer/edelstahl-duschwannen-duschtassen-fuer-wohnmobile/
